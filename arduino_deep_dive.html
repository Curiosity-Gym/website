We are hosting a deep dive workshop on arduino !!

The workshop will be conducted over a course of 2 weeks on weekdays and saturdays, totalling upto 18 hrs of accelerated Arduino learning.

Dates : 7th June to 23 June
Timing : 6 pm to 8 pm on weekdays, 10 AM to 12:00 PM on Saturdays

The objective of the workshop is to introduce the participants to the universe of arduino and explore the different possibilities with arduino.

Participants will be provided Arduino Uno boards and a custom built shield for learning and executing arduino programs.

Participants will be introduced to the basics of electronics to begin with, followed by Introduction to arduino, and then its usage. They will be taught about different sensors and actuators in depth, their interfaces, the best practices to be followed while using them and walk through with Code examples for each.

There will be hands-on sessions with a variety of sensors and actuators throughout the course of the workshop.
