Career Opportunities

Curiosity Gym is a place for people of all ages to allow for sparking of one’s own curiosity through applied learning, tinkering, prototyping or making. The Gym offers a shared learning environment for all ages through workshops, talks and fun projects that anyone can participate in.

We are looking for zealous, restless and curious individuals who believe in inquiry-based learning, learning-by-doing, tinkering, creative expression and learning through collaborative teamwork. A wide variety of skill-sets will be nurtured at the Gym: design, electronics, science, mathematics, social sciences, philosophy, crafts, music, photography, programming and entrepreneurship. If you love what you do, love to ideate, design, teach or mentor, Curiosity Gym is the place for you.

While there are Gyms for exercise of the body, temples for religion and other organisations to find your spirit, Curiosity Gym is a place for you to catalyse your own curiosity and imagine your next question.


Who can apply?
Graduates and/or current students of B.E./ B.Tech./ BCA/ B.Sc./ MBA/ BBA from any stream, with the required skills can apply. (Applicants pursuing their respective Bachelor degrees will be provided with internship opportunity)


Compensation and Location
We offer a competitive compensation package commensurate with age & experience. Role is based in Mumbai, India.


How to Apply?
Please send your CV to explore@curiositygym.com. Please subject your email "Job Description Title : Last Name_First Name".
