Career Opportunities

Curiosity Gym is a place for people of all ages to allow for sparking of one’s own curiosity through applied learning, tinkering, prototyping or making. The Gym offers a shared learning environment for all ages through workshops, talks and fun projects that anyone can participate in.

We are looking for zealous, restless and curious individuals who believe in inquiry-based learning, learning-by-doing, tinkering, creative expression and learning through collaborative teamwork. A wide variety of skill-sets will be nurtured at the Gym: design, electronics, science, mathematics, social sciences, philosophy, crafts, music, photography, programming and entrepreneurship. If you love what you do, love to ideate, design, teach or mentor, Curiosity Gym is the place for you.

While there are Gyms for exercise of the body, temples for religion and other organisations to find your spirit, Curiosity Gym is a place for you to catalyse your own curiosity and imagine your next question.


Who can apply?
Graduates and/or current students of B.E./ B.Tech./ BCA/ B.Sc./ MBA/ BBA from any stream, with the required skills can apply. (Applicants pursuing their respective Bachelor degrees will be provided with internship opportunity)


Compensation and Location
We offer a competitive compensation package commensurate with age & experience. Role is based in Mumbai, India.


How to Apply?

Innovation Instructor

Looking for an interesting job as an Educator in an Innovative Hub in schools/colleges?

In which field you ask?

Any field in the areas of Science, Technology, Engineering, Art or Math (STEAM).

Our curriculum is broad based and seeks to expose students to the various aspects of STEAM. So a candidate can have a BS/MS/PhD in any field. Main criteria of selection is to be passionate about teaching, learning and connecting with and inspiring others.
Candidates must be committed to strengthening and enriching student learning through innovative teaching, research the latest on a topic and be able to simplify concepts to the level of a novice/intermediate learner. Must also be willing and able to reach out to students of varied aptitudes. Ability to build strong interpersonal relationships with students, parents and colleagues a plus.

  • Must have excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Must have ability to work in a dynamic, fluid environment.
  • Previous experience working in innovative teaching programs a plus.
  • Must be able to help students develop analytical and critical thinking skills.

If you’re a seasoned educator with experience learning new curriculum, we’ll provide the training and tools to hone your expertise in new subject matter.
If you’re passionate about any aspect of STEAM and have always wanted to share your knowledge with tomorrow’s innovators, we’ve got the job for you.

Full-time, part-time and internship positions available

Compensation will be commensurate with experience, academic qualification and job role.
Position is based in Mumbai.

  • 0 - 1 years experience
