About us

Vision

Curiosity Gym is a place open for people of all ages to allow for sparking of one’s own curiosity through applied learning tinkering, prototyping and making.

The Gym allows for a mental and physical space for taking one’s curiosity to the next level, and hopes to foster the ability to ‘learn to learn’ by offering a shared learning environment for all.

Visitors and Whyers (members of the Gym so called, since they are passionate about asking Why) will be able to take part in a variety of workshops, projects, exhibits, games, competitions, talks and exchange ideas, opinions with others on a wide variety of subjects.

Curiosity Gym is a platform for both participants and facilitators to flexibly define desired learning experiences, objectives and outcomes.

Constant, iterative learning for participants, while having fun is a key goal of the Gym.

Objective

Through Curiosity Gym we aspire to:

  • Encourage inquiry, discovery, learning, design, collaboration and experimentation
  • Develop programmes that combine disciplines to achieve learning objectives
  • Create programs that encourage collaborative working together of all age groups
  • Strengthen the fundamental concepts of topics in an open, hands-on, collaborative environment
  • Enhance learning attained at schools, colleges and corporates by offering an “always on” environment to experiment
  • Create a platform for exchange of ideas and open debate
  • Showcase uses of the some of the latest technologies in a simple manner
  • Enable useful and constructive use of child and adult free time by helping connect with mentors in field of interest
  • All of the above while ensuring the learning process is fun

Motivation

We have had the feeling for a long while, that a place to go to, so as to get help on an idea or a project, be guided and exposed to people of different backgrounds and ages, and an association of novices, amateurs and experts in different fields was much missing in Mumbai and actually most places in the world. Curiosity Gym has been setup to address this need felt by many.

So, whether you are a student needing some help on a project, wanting to learn why or how something works and would like to build your own device/drone/train or anything else, a youth looking to find your career by meeting with people who have done something in your prospective field before or a budding entrepreneur looking for mentoring help on design or manufacturing or marketing or scaling your startup or a stay at home mom, business person or a retiree looking to learn some new technology and meet interesting people, Curiosity Gym is a place to come to.

While there are Gyms for exercise of the body and temples for your religion and other organisations to find your spirit, Curiosity Gym is a place for you to catalyse your own curiosity and imagine your next question.
